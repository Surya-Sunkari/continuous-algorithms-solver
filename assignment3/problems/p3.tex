\section*{Problem 3}

Let $R \geq 0$ and $\mathbf{v} \in \mathbb{R}^d_{\geq 0}$. Give an algorithm which runs in time $O(d \log d)$, and computes\footnote{Note that this is one step in the projected gradient descent algorithm in Section 3, Part XII. It may be helpful to consider the Karush-Kuhn-Tucker conditions for this constrained optimization problem.}
\[
\operatorname{argmin}_{\|\mathbf{x}\|_1 \leq R} \left\{ \frac{1}{2} \|\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{v}\|_2^2 \right\}.
\]
